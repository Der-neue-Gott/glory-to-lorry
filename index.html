Story about us

Our company was founded in 2020. We specialize in cargo transportation from many other countries and their capitals, we cooperate with sellers on AliExpress, government and social organizations. We also work with many logistics parks and centers in Kyrgyzstan, such as "Kyrgyzprodukt" and "Dordoi".

Our workplaces are equipped with modern software.
Our warehouses use a modern automated system. All cargo is under reliable protection.
Our team works around the clock on supply lines.
